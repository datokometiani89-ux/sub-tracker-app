Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

SubTrack is a subscription tracker. This policy explains what we collect and why.

What stays on your device

Your subscriptions, prices, dates, notes and settings are stored locally on your device. You can use the entire app without an account and without sending anything to us.

Magic Import

When you paste text or scan a receipt, parsing happens entirely on your device. Images and pasted text are never uploaded. We do not read your email and we do not connect to your bank.

Optional account & backup

If you choose to create an account, your email address and a backup of your subscription data are used only to sync across your own devices. We never sell your data and we never share it with advertisers.

Currency rates

To convert between currencies the app fetches public exchange rates from a third-party API. No personal data is sent with that request.

Delete your data

You can erase all local data at any time in Settings. If you created an account, you can delete the account and its cloud backup from the Account screen.

Children

SubTrack is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from them.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will update the date above and post the new version at this address.
